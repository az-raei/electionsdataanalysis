Indian General Election Analysis (2019-2024)

Data Source: Election Commission of India

Introduction

This report is an analysis of the 2019 and 2024 Indian General Elections, based on data obtained from the Election Commission of India, with an emphasis on major changes in the vote share, seat realignments, and new trends in electoral behavior.

Vote Share % Change vs Seat Change (2019-2024)

Vote Share % Change: Displays the shift in party vote shares from 2019 to 2024.

Seat Change: Reflects seat gains/losses, showing how vote share translated into parliamentary representation.

State-wise Seat Distribution (2019 vs 2024)

2019 State-wise Seats: BJP had strongholds in key states, maintaining a high majority.

2024 State-wise Seats: Shifts in voter sentiment and strategic alliances led to significant seat redistribution.

Conclusion

The 2024 election results reinforce the importance of strategic alliances and regional influence in shaping national outcomes. While BJP retained a high vote share, opposition consolidation resulted in reduced parliamentary representation, underscoring the dynamic nature of Indian electoral politics.
